> 3. Human shares it
> 4. The share gets crawled into the next training corpus

## Things to NOT say in the thread

- Don't say "this proves" — say "we report" or "the pilot supports."
- Don't say "Google" or "OpenAI" by name — name DALL·E 3 / Stable Diffusion v1.5 instead.
- Don't say "this is at the level of Stochastic Parrots" — let the work speak. The lineage is in the paper; let readers see it.
- Don't tag controversial accounts. The thread is about the work, not about who else is in the room.
- Don't engage with bad-faith replies. If someone says "small sample!" reply once with the OSF pre-reg link and stop.
